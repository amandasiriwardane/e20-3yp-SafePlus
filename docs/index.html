Welcome to Safe Plus

Smart Safety Helmet for Industrial Workers

Introduction

Industrial workplaces, such as construction sites, factories, and mining zones, show significant safety risks to workers due to environmental hazards, heavy machinery, and unpredictable conditions. Safe Plus introduces an advanced smart safety helmet that integrates real-time monitoring, impact detection, and emergency alert systems to ensure worker safety.

Our Features

Safe Plus comes with a range of advanced features to support worker safety and productivity.

Health Monitoring

Monitors heart rate and temperature to detect potential health issues early.

Smart GPS

Tracks worker location in real-time to ensure safety during operations.

Alert Notifications

Sends real-time alerts for anomalies, ensuring quick response.

Solution Architecture

The architecture of Safe Plus integrates IoT sensors, cloud analytics, and user interfaces for effective safety monitoring.

Hardware & Software

The integration of advanced hardware components and robust software frameworks ensures a reliable and efficient system.

HARDWARE COMPONENTS

  • Microcontroller (ESP32)
  • Heart Rate Sensor (MAX 30102)
  • GPS Module (NEO-6M)
  • Temperature Sensor (MAX 30205)
  • Accelerometer (MPU 6050)

SOFTWARE FRAMEWORKS

  • React: Frontend Development
  • Express.js: Backend Framework
  • Node.js: Server Management
  • MongoDB: Database Management
  • AWS: Cloud Management
