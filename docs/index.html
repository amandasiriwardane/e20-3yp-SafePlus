Welcome to Safe Plus

Safe Plus - Smart Safety Helmet

Introduction

Industrial workplaces—construction sites, factories, and mining zones—pose significant risks due to heavy machinery, hazardous gases, and unpredictable environments. Each year, many accidents result in severe injuries, fatalities, and financial losses due to downtime and medical costs.

Safe Plus offers a revolutionary smart safety helmet integrating real-time monitoring, impact detection, and emergency alerting. Through advanced sensor systems and wireless connectivity, it enhances worker protection and enables immediate supervisor alerts during hazardous situations.
