INTRODUCTION

Industrial workplaces—construction sites, factories, and mining zones—face significant challenges due to heavy machinery, hazardous environments, and unpredictable conditions. Daily, accidents result in injuries, fatalities, and huge financial losses.

Safe Plus is designed to tackle these challenges by merging innovative sensor technology with wireless connectivity. Our smart safety helmet continuously monitors environmental conditions, detects impacts, and triggers emergency alerts, ensuring immediate response and enhanced worker safety.
